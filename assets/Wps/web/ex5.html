MOTILAL NEHRU GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE

HISTORY

MNGPC - Motilal Nehru Government Polytechnic College is located in Pondicherry, And it is affiliated with Directorate of Technical Education Chennai. The college was established in the year 1961 to promote quality technical education that not only educates the students but make them qualified and strong enough to meet the increasing demands of the society.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS

  • Good Exposure to students on all fronts
  • Excellent Placement record since inception
  • Job oriented internship programs are provided
